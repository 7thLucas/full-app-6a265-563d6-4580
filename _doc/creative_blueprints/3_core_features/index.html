Core Features

Everything you need to turn video into structured intelligence.

Content Creators Marketers & Brand Teams Researchers & Educators

Smart Upload & Ingest

Drag-and-drop or paste a URL. Supports MP4, MOV, WebM, and AVI with cloud processing.

AI Content Summary

Generates concise, human-readable summaries covering narrative, key topics, and structure.

Scene & Moment Detection

Auto-segments video into scenes with timestamps, thumbnails, and descriptive labels.

Object & Activity Recognition

Identifies people, objects, actions, and text overlays frame by frame.

Transcript & Captions

Accurate transcripts with speaker diarization. Export as SRT or VTT caption files.

Sentiment & Tone Analysis

Evaluates emotional tone across the timeline — positive, negative, neutral — with visual graphs.
