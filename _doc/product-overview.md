# Video Analyzer

## What it is
Video Analyzer is an AI-powered web application that lets users upload or link videos and receive instant, intelligent analysis — including content summaries, scene breakdowns, object and activity detection, transcript generation, and sentiment insights. It turns raw video into structured, searchable, actionable intelligence.

## Who it's for

- **Content Creators** — YouTubers, social media managers, and video producers who need to understand what's in their footage quickly, generate metadata, and optimize content for engagement.
- **Marketers & Brand Teams** — Professionals analyzing ad performance, competitor video content, and brand-sentiment across video campaigns.
- **Researchers & Educators** — Academics, journalists, and trainers who need to catalog, search, and cite specific moments within large video libraries.

## Core capabilities

1. **Smart Upload & Ingest** — Drag-and-drop upload or paste a URL; supports common formats (MP4, MOV, WebM, AVI). Videos are processed in the cloud.
2. **AI Content Summary** — Generates a concise, human-readable summary of the video's narrative, key topics, and structure.
3. **Scene & Moment Detection** — Automatically segments the video into scenes with timestamps, thumbnails, and descriptive labels.
4. **Object & Activity Recognition** — Identifies people, objects, actions, text overlays, and on-screen elements frame by frame.
5. **Transcript & Caption Generation** — Produces accurate transcripts with speaker diarization and exportable caption files (SRT/VTT).
6. **Sentiment & Tone Analysis** — Evaluates the emotional tone across the video timeline — positive, negative, neutral — with visual graphs.
7. **Search Within Video** — Full-text and visual search across all analyzed content; jump to the exact moment.

## Positioning & tone
Video Analyzer is positioned as a professional-grade yet accessible tool — powerful AI under a clean, calm interface. The tone is confident, modern, and no-nonsense: "Upload. Analyze. Understand." The brand conveys precision, clarity, and speed.

## Branding

- **Primary color**: #2563EB (vivid blue — trust, intelligence)
- **Secondary color**: #0F172A (slate-900 — authority, depth)
- **Accent color**: #F59E0B (amber — energy, key highlights)
- **Typography style**: Clean sans-serif display headings, readable body text

## Key benefits

- **Save hours** — Reduce manual video review time by up to 90%.
- **Surface hidden insights** — Discover patterns, sentiment shifts, and details humans miss at speed.
- **Searchable video library** — Every frame, word, and object becomes findable.
- **Export anywhere** — Summaries, transcripts, and metadata export to JSON, CSV, SRT, and PDF.

## Scope
MVP is a single-user web app focused on upload → analyze → explore. No collaborative workspaces or real-time streaming analysis in v1.
